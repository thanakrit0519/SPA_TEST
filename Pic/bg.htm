Bliss is the name of the default computer wallpaper of Microsoft's Windows XP operating system. It is an unedited photograph of a green hill and blue sky with clouds in the Los Carneros American Viticultural Area of Sonoma County, California, United States. Charles O'Rear originally sent Bliss to Corbis in 1996, and Microsoft bought the rights to the picture in 2000.
